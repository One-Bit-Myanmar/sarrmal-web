SarrMal

Food Recommendation AI

The app features a unique image recognition tool that allows users to take pictures of their meals and instantly receive detailed nutritional information, including calorie counts. Our AI-driven food recommendation system is designed to promote healthy eating habits by providing personalized meal suggestions and calorie calculations.

Purpose

  • Enable users to take control of their diet with AI-driven insights, helping them achieve a healthier lifestyle.

Product Features

  • Personalized Meal Recommendation
  • Using user data like age, weight, height, and dietary preferences, our AI creates personalized meal plans for a balanced, healthy diet.
  • Calories Calculation
  • The app calculates calories for recommended and consumed meals, helping users stay on track with their dietary goals.
  • Image Recognition
  • Users can take pictures of their food, and the app will analyze the image to provide calorie information and nutritional content.
